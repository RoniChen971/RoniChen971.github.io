Our Vision

Aye is a breakthrough in how people prove who they are online.

In 2024, identity fraud rose by 244% due to deepfakes and sophisticated social engineering. Passwords and multi-factor authentication (MFA) is no longer enough. Traditional logins are outdated. MFA is clunky, hackable, and built for a digital world that no longer exists.

At Aye, we envision a digital world where trust is continuous, identity is secure, and fraud is a thing of the past. We believe every person deserves to move through the digital world freely - without fear of impersonation, breaches, or complex logins.
That’s why we’ve built a new kind of Digital ID - a living identity that constantly adapts to who you are, where you are, and how you behave.

It’s not what you remember. It’s who you are.
That’s the new standard of trust.
